WEATHER

About

I build this application using the CoreLocation and Open weather API. This app is a clean and minimal weather application which shows you the weather forecast of your current location. And you can also get the other city weather just by entering the city name in the input box (TextField).

The data's showed are Temparature in celcius, weather condition, sunrise and sunset time in IST(Indian Standard Time). And the bottom half of the screen will give you temperatures for every hour in one day.
